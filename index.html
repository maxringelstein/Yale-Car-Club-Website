About YCAR

About YCAR

Yale Car Club was founded in 2026 to bring together students, alumni, and industry professionals around a shared interest in cars. We organize events throughout the year — from casual meetups to curated car shows — and serve as a hub for automotive culture on campus.

Open to everyone at Yale, regardless of what you drive or how much you know. The only requirement is an interest in cars.
